# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Apprendre le Creole respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
- This app does not require account creation.
- This app does not collect personal data on its own backend.
- Progress can be stored locally in your browser/app storage.

## Microphone Use
- The app may request microphone access for speaking practice.
- Audio is used on-device for recording practice and is not uploaded by default.

## Third-Party Services
- Browser/device speech services may be used for text-to-speech.
- App stores (Google Play, Apple App Store) may collect analytics according to their own policies.
